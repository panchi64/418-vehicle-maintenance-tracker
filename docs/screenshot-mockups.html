Production Notes

Screenshot Dimensions

Final screenshots should be 1290 × 2796 px (iPhone 15 Pro Max / 6.9"). Apple auto-scales to smaller devices. These mockups show the compositional layout — final production should use actual app screenshots with caption overlays.

Caption Placement

Captions sit above the device frame, not overlaid on the screen. This keeps the UI clean and readable at thumbnail size. Use JetBrains Mono or SF Mono for caption text to match the brand.

Device Frame

Use a minimal device frame with sharp edges (no rounded corners) to match the brutalist aesthetic. Consider no frame at all — just the screen content with a 2px border. This would be distinctive in the App Store.

Background

Screenshots should use the app's cerulean blue (#0033BE) as the background behind/around the device frame. This creates a cohesive visual identity and stands out against the App Store's white/light gray listing page.

First Three Priority

Screenshots 1–3 are conversion-critical. They must be immediately legible as thumbnails. Test by viewing at 30% zoom — if you can't read the caption and understand the screen's purpose, simplify.
