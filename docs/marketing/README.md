# Checkpoint — Marketing Foundation

> Everything required to launch Checkpoint with a credible Puerto Rico–first push and a clear path to scale beyond it.

This folder is the **source of truth** for marketing. Product docs live one level up in `docs/`. Anything visible to a non-user (the App Store, press, social, ads, the website) traces back to a decision recorded here.

## How to use this kit

1. **Founder reads `LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md` end-to-end once** — it sets vocabulary every other doc reuses (e.g. "marbete moment," "Casual / Enthusiast / Diaspora," "Phase 0/1/2/3").
2. **Lock the ASO copy in `APP_STORE_ASSETS.md` before any other work** — keywords compound, screenshots are the conversion lever, and these change least.
3. **Pick 2 campaigns from `CAMPAIGNS.md` for the first 90 days.** Resist running all of them. The ones to start with are flagged.
4. **Use `PRESS_KIT.md` outreach templates as-is** — they're written for Puerto Rico tech press (El Nuevo Día Negocios, Sin Comillas, Estamos en Línea) and US iOS press (MacStories, 9to5Mac, The Sweet Setup).

## What this kit deliberately does NOT do

- **No paid acquisition budget yet.** Checkpoint at launch is a single-developer indie. Every campaign in this playbook can be executed with $0–$500 and the developer's own time. A paid plan is sketched in `LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md` for after Phase 2.
- **No social-media calendar past 90 days.** Calendars decay. The playbook defines the *system* (pillars, cadence, themes); the founder fills the calendar week to week.
- **No App Store screenshot images.** The mockups in `APP_STORE_ASSETS.md` are **spec sheets** — exact layouts, copy, colors, and source screens. Render them in Figma or use Screenshots.pro / Rotato; do not hand them to a designer without the spec.

## Decisions (resolved)

These were tagged ⚠️ during drafting. They're locked now; record kept for posterity in `LAUNCH_PLAYBOOK.md` § 8.

- **Launch name in App Store**: ✅ `Checkpoint: Car Maintenance` (27 chars).
- **Bilingual at launch?** ✅ Yes — Spanish (Mexico) locale at launch alongside English. General-LATAM voice, **not PR-coded** in the App Store listing itself.
- **Marbete-cycle launch spike timing**: ✅ Late July — DTOP press history shows extension events specifically for July/August marbete renewals, and the lingering cultural "July marbete" muscle memory means the term still spikes in search even though renewals are now staggered. January is the credible secondary anchor.
- **First press exclusive**: ✅ El Nuevo Día Negocios (PR-press lane). US-iOS-press exclusive (MacStories vs The Sweet Setup) is a separate decision still open in `PRESS_KIT.md` — different beat, not in competition.
- **Spanish review gate**: ✅ Two native PR Spanish speakers from the founder's network of close friends / coworkers, informal read-aloud pass. No paid translator required.
